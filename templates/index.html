YouTube Video Downloader

By Darsh Gupta

How to use:

Using this web app is simple. Just get the ID of the video (eg. ID of https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ is dQw4w9WgXcQ), copy it, and in the URL bar, paste the ID after a / after the domain name (eg. https://www.youtubeviddownloader.com/dQw4w9WgXcQ), and hit enter. Then wait for your video to download.
